New HTML5 Elements Used:

  • Details - An element that hides or shows additional details on demand. Has an open attribute to specify wether the element should be visible to the user. The details tag is supported in all major browsers except IE.
  • Summary - A child element of details. Used to show a summary of the details that stays visible. The summary has no specific attributes to it. The details tag is supported in all major browsers except IE.

Hawaii

I have been to Hawaii for a wrestling tournament when I was younger. I'm not sure if it was just where I was at the time but I really like that everything was quite and closed by 9 p.m. but everyone was up and going early which I enjoy more. I also enjoyed the weather because even when it rained it was still warm out.

Las Vegas

Me and my wife enjoy going to vegas every couple years. We have seen various shows/events when we've gone including a UFC card as well as a Cirque du Soleil which I would highly recommend. My favourite restaurant that we always make sure to go to at least once per trip is Mon Ami Gabi located in Paris Hotel and Casino where I ironically get the burger as it's the best burger I've ever had.
